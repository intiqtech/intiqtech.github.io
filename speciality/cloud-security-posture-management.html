Cloud Security Posture Management (CSPM & CWPP)

CCSPM & CWPP

At Intiq Technology, we're dedicated to enhancing your cloud security with a tailored approach that encompasses Cloud Security Posture Management (CSPM) and Cloud Workload Protection Platform (CWPP). We understand that in today's dynamic cloud landscape, a personalized security strategy is essential.

Intiq Technology

CSPM takes center stage in our approach, allowing us to meticulously assess and uphold the security posture of your organization's cloud resources. Our team works diligently to identify and swiftly remediate any misconfigurations, vulnerabilities, or potential security gaps across various cloud platforms. This proactive stance ensures that your cloud environment remains fortified against evolving threats.

Intiq Technology

In tandem, our implementation of CWPP provides a layer of protection that zeroes in on safeguarding the very workloads driving your cloud operations. Whether it's virtual machines, containers, or serverless functions, we prioritize security controls that shield these critical workloads from both known and emerging threats. With our CWPP strategy in place, you can operate with confidence, knowing that your workloads are shielded by a comprehensive defense mechanism.
