Azure Sentinel

At Intiq Technology we help organizations design and deploy SIEM tools like Azure Sentinel which offers the capability to seamlessly collect and aggregate security events and logs from diverse sources, encompassing network devices, cloud services, on-premises systems, and third-party applications. This centralized data collection empowers our security team with enhanced visibility into potential threats and activities across our entire infrastructure.

Intiq Technology

With Azure Sentinel, our skilled security analysts can delve into incident investigations with precision. By correlating data from various sources and timelines, they gain the ability to uncover the underlying causes of incidents swiftly. This comprehensive approach aids in understanding the full scope of a breach, allowing us to take effective actions to mitigate risks and minimize the impact on our operations.
