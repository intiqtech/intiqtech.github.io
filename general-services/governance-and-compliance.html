Governance And Compliance

Intiq Technology specializes in assisting organizations in formulating corporate security policies. We achieve this through planning, designing, and implementing governance strategies that align seamlessly with industry standards.

Our valuable offerings include practical governance guidelines, drawn from the wealth of experience our customers have found highly beneficial. These blueprints empower CISOs, Cloud Architects and Infrastructure teams to establish a consistent array of cloud resources, ensuring alignment with industry standards, prevalent patterns, and essential requirements.

Our blueprints and templates provide vital support for organizations in managing the following key aspects:

  • Role assignments
  • Policy assignments
  • Resource Manager templates
  • Resource groups
